How It Works

Four simple steps to delete all your tweets.

1

Download & Unzip

Download the ZIP file and extract it to any folder on your computer.

Open chrome://extensions, enable Developer Mode, click Load unpacked, and select the WipeX folder.

3

Go to Your Profile

Open X/Twitter and navigate to your profile page so WipeX can find your tweets.

4

Click Start Wiping

Open the WipeX popup, choose a speed mode, and hit Start Wiping. Done!
