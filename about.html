About

Built to help you clean your digital past

WipeX is a free, privacy-first Chrome extension created because I personally needed a reliable way to wipe my Twitter history — and existing tools were either paid, sketchy, or broken.

🎯 Mission

Everyone deserves a fresh start online. WipeX gives you full control over your X/Twitter content without requiring any API keys, account sign-ups, or monthly fees. Everything runs directly in your browser — no data ever leaves your device.

📖 The Story

I wanted to clean my Twitter account but every tool I found either cost money, required dodgy OAuth access, or stopped working after rate limits. So I built WipeX myself — a simple Chrome extension that uses the same actions as a human user, just automated. It grew into a collection of tools to help people manage their X presence.
